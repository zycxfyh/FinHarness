# Musings

Loose ideas live here before they are ready to become engineering plans,
research modules, or product requirements.

Working title:

```text
Little Thoughts
```

Avoid treating these notes as finished truth. Their job is to preserve useful
sparks, rough models, questions, and half-formed frameworks until they either:

1. become a real project module,
2. become a research note,
3. get merged into a clearer framework, or
4. get retired.

## Capture Rule

When an idea is not yet engineering-ready, capture it here with:

```text
Question:
Raw thought:
Why it might matter:
What would make it testable:
Possible future use:
```

This keeps the thought alive without pretending it is already a system.
