  [Program #277](https://github.com/zycxfyh/FinHarness/issues/277);

- runtime and capability status:
  source code, tests, Taskfile, route graph, models, and generated system views.

Do not restore a maintained Markdown table of current workstreams, an immediate
concurrency plan, preallocated slice sequence, or copied child Issue status.
Historical direction may remain useful context, but it cannot authorize work or
prove current capability.
